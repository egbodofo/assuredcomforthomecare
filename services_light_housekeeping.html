Light housekeeping

We understand that quality and compassionate in-home assistance, or Personal Care Services, can enrich an individual’s quality of life, and provide the support that makes aging at home possible for those who just need a helping hand.

Our Light housekeeping

Together, you and your Caregiver, will develop a customized Personal Care Plan, which could range from service needs for only a couple of hours a week, to daily or live-in support. Your Caregiver will engage in ongoing conversations with you to ensure your needs are being met at all times.
Our highly-skilled Caregivers are background-checked, worker’s compensation-insured and bonded, to not only ensure your security, but to deliver the high-quality care you deserve.
